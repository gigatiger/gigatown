This is a lore reference for Dezdemona, my main oc that has been around for a long time. This year I actually got a fursuit of him, my first professionally made fursuit head. I've actually amde three for him in the past, and they were fun projects. Anyways, this is Dezdemona. He's a chimera, specifically a mix of a dog, dragon, and a ram, He's fun, his muzzle shape and breed tends to change based on who's drawing him and I've never minded. Originally I entended for him to be a cane corso, and then a chihuahua. He used to have a more saturated color scheme, but after a rework by an artist named Novembertoons he has a softer color scheme that I adore.

This ref revolves around his origins as a sorcerer in the Medieval ages, eventually infiltrating a sacred council of practitioners in order to weild chaos magic. It's one of my more cartoonishly edgy fictional worlds, but I hope to write more about it soon.
